Recovery Support

Resources and guidance for individuals in recovery. Here you can find help and connect with others.

Recovery Support Systems and Helplines:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255) - Provides support for individuals in crisis or considering suicide.
  • SAMHSA National Helpline: 1-800-662-HELP (4357) - Offers free, confidential treatment referral and information in English and Spanish for individuals and families facing mental health and/or substance use disorders.
  • NAMI Helpline (National Alliance on Mental Illness): 1-800-950-NAMI (6264) - Provides information, referrals, and support to individuals and families affected by mental health conditions.
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741 - Offers free, confidential support via text message for individuals in crisis.
  • Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) Disaster Distress Helpline: 1-800-985-5990 - Provides crisis counseling and support to people experiencing emotional distress related to natural or human-caused disasters.
  • TrevorLifeline (for LGBTQ+ Youth): 1-866-488-7386 - Offers crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to LGBTQ+ youth.
  • Trans Lifeline (for Transgender Individuals): 1-877-565-8860 - Provides crisis support and resources specifically for transgender individuals.
  • Veterans Crisis Line: 1-800-273-8255, Press 1 - Offers free, confidential support to veterans in crisis and their families.
  • Postpartum Support International Helpline: 1-800-944-4773 - Provides support, information, and resources for individuals experiencing postpartum depression and other perinatal mood disorders.
  • Eating Disorders Helpline (National Eating Disorders Association): 1-800-931-2237 - Offers support, resources, and treatment referrals for individuals affected by eating disorders.
