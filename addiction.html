Addiction Support

Types of Addiction:

  • Substance Addiction: This includes addiction to drugs or alcohol.
  • Behavioral Addiction: Examples include addiction to gambling, gaming, or shopping.
  • Food Addiction: Addiction to certain types of food, often characterized by compulsive overeating.
  • Internet Addiction: Addiction to the internet, including excessive use of social media or online gaming.

Signs of Addiction:

  • Increased tolerance to the substance or behavior.
  • Withdrawal symptoms when unable to engage in the substance or behavior.
  • Loss of interest in activities that were once enjoyed.
  • Continued use despite negative consequences.
  • Secrecy or lying about the extent of use.
  • Financial problems related to acquiring the substance or engaging in the behavior.

What to Do If You Suspect You're Addicted:

  • Seek professional help from a therapist or counselor specializing in addiction.
  • Consider joining a support group such as Alcoholics Anonymous (AA) or Narcotics Anonymous (NA).
  • Reach out to friends and family for support and encouragement.
  • Practice self-care techniques such as exercise, meditation, and healthy eating.
  • Avoid triggers and situations that may lead to relapse.
  • Be patient with yourself and recognize that recovery is a journey.
